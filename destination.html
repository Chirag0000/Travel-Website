Paris

$2500/week for 1 Person





Paris, the enchanting capital of France, is a city that effortlessly blends history, culture, and modernity. Known as the "City of Lights," it captivates visitors with its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. The romantic ambiance along the Seine River and charming cobblestone streets create a timeless allure. Renowned for its world-class cuisine, Paris offers a gastronomic journey through quaint cafes and Michelin-starred restaurants. Art and fashion flourish in its galleries and boutiques, while the lively atmosphere of Montmartre and vibrant neighborhoods showcase the city's dynamic spirit. Paris remains an eternal muse, inviting exploration and inspiration.

Kerala

$1500/week for 1 Person





Kerala, a captivating state in southwestern India, is renowned for its lush landscapes, serene backwaters, and vibrant culture. Nestled between the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats, it boasts palm-fringed beaches, tranquil houseboat journeys in Alleppey, and rich biodiversity in its tropical forests. Known as "God's Own Country," Kerala showcases traditional dance forms, Ayurvedic wellness, and colorful festivals. The scent of spices lingers in bustling markets, and tea plantations adorn the misty hills of Munnar. With a harmonious blend of tradition and modernity, Kerala captivates visitors with its scenic beauty, warm hospitality, and a unique cultural tapestry.

Bali

$2800/week for 1 Person





Bali, a tropical paradise in Indonesia, enchants visitors with its vibrant culture, lush landscapes, and pristine beaches. Known for its rich spiritual heritage, the island boasts intricate temples like Uluwatu and Besakih. Bali's lush terraced rice fields and dense jungles create a picturesque backdrop, while the iconic Tegallalang Rice Terraces showcase the island's agricultural beauty. Visitors can indulge in vibrant markets, savor Balinese cuisine, and experience traditional dance performances. The crystal-clear waters surrounding Bali offer world-class diving and surfing opportunities. With its warm hospitality and serene ambiance, Bali remains a haven for those seeking relaxation, adventure, and a glimpse into Balinese traditions.

Las Vegas

$4000/week for 1 Person





Las Vegas, often dubbed the "Entertainment Capital of the World," is a vibrant city in the heart of the Nevada desert. Known for its iconic Strip, adorned with dazzling lights and world-renowned resorts, Las Vegas is a global hub of entertainment, gaming, and nightlife. From grand casinos and captivating live shows to gourmet dining and themed attractions, the city offers an electrifying blend of excitement. Beyond the glitz, visitors can explore nearby natural wonders, including the stunning Red Rock Canyon. With its unique blend of glamour and desert charm, Las Vegas promises an unforgettable experience for those seeking thrills, entertainment, and non-stop excitement.

Toronto

$1000/week for 1 Person





Toronto, the vibrant metropolis on the shores of Lake Ontario, blends cultural diversity with urban sophistication. Renowned for its iconic skyline, the CN Tower, and bustling financial district, Toronto offers a dynamic mix of art, entertainment, and culinary experiences. Stroll through diverse neighborhoods like Kensington Market and enjoy global cuisines. Explore world-class museums such as the Royal Ontario Museum and AGO. The city hosts major events like the Toronto International Film Festival, reflecting its status as a cultural hub. With lush parks, waterfront trails, and a welcoming atmosphere, Toronto embraces its multicultural identity, making it a must-visit destination.
